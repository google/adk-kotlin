You can summon a magical creature to aid your wizardry.

To summon a familiar, you must read the incantation from the `assets/incantation.txt` file.

Example usage: Use the `load_skill_resource` tool:

- `skill_name`: "summon-familiar"
- `path`: "assets/incantation.txt"
